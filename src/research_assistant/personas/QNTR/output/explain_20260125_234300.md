## 2. Theoretical Foundation
DRO rests on **stochastic optimization and robust decision theory**, with the foundational equation:

\[
\min_{x \in \mathcal{X}} \sup_{P \in \mathcal{P}} \mathbb{E}_{P}[\ell(x, \xi)]
\]

Here, \(x\) denotes decision variables in feasible set \(\mathcal{X}\), \(\mathcal{P}\) is the ambiguity set of distributions, \(\ell(x, \xi)\) is the loss function, and \(\xi\) captures uncertainty[6].
